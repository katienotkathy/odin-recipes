Ramen

Description

A tantilizing bowl of tsukemen ramen. Learn to make it and watch all of your problems melt away! Yum!

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Tsukemen broth (don't forget the garlic!)
  • Chashu pork
  • Marinated egg
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Seaweed wrap
  • Corn kernels
  • Other toppings according to preference

Steps

  1. The night before, hard boil your desired number of eggs and leave them to soak overnight in a marinade
  2. Make broth, bring to boil
  3. A few minutes before the broth is ready, boil the noodles in a separate pot. They should be al dente
  4. Transfer noodles and broth to bowls (keeping separate)
  5. Add toppings to broth
  6. Enjoy!
