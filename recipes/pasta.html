Ramen

Description

A delicious bowl of pasta, with sausage for protein, and one or more servings of vegetables. Depends on how much you eat, and you're not going to want to stop!

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Sweet italian sausage
  • Jar of pasta sauce or sauce made from scatch (if you're fancy)
  • Heavy cream
  • Veggies to taste, we recommend a bit of spinach and peas

Steps

  1. Set a pot of water to boil, prepare to boil pasta about ten minutes before sauce is finished
  2. Remove skin from sausage, put it pan and mash into pieces according to preference
  3. Brown sausage until mostly cooked
  4. Poor pasta sauce on top
  5. Add heavy cream to taste, at least 1/4 cup
  6. Add veggies
  7. Heat sauce until it bubbles
  8. Combine pasta with sauce
  9. Enjoy!
