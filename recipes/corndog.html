Ramen

Description

You've had plenty of vendor corndogs or microwaved frozen corndogs, but have you ever made your own? I haven't either, but I'll take a guess at what it might take to make one below.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog, cooked
  • Cornbread mix
  • Deep fryer or enough oil in a deep pan to submerge hotdog
  • Desired toppings, we recommend ketchup and mustard

Steps

  1. Mix cornbread according to instructions
  2. Get exterior of cooked (but not yet heated hotdog) wet
  3. Roll the hotdog around in the cornbread mixture
  4. Fry the hotdog until cornbread mixture rises and cooks (?? lol)
  5. Repeat for desired number of corndogs
  6. Poor desired toppings, let cool
  7. Enjoy!
